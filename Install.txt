Tritanium Apple Databases
--
These Databases are required by TA and can be downloaded from 
http://wiki.eve-id.net/CCP_Database_Dump_Resources

invTypes - items [Items]
invMarketGroups - Self-linking tree of market groups; use instead of groups and categories [MarketGroups]
invMetaGroups - used with invMetaGroups to find variants [MetaGroups]
invMetaTypes - items of same invMetaTypes.metaGroupID are similar [MetaTypes]

invBlueprintTypes - blueprints [Blueprints]
invTypeMaterials - materials required for manufacture and reprocessing [BlueprintMaterials]
ramTypeRequirements - additional items required by blueprints [BlueprintRams]?
